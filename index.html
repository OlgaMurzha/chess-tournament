Welcome to Chess Tournament 2024!

The Tournament was founded in 2016 and since that time takes place every year. It's a good opportunity to face rivals offline, feel the true spirit of a competition, and learn new people.

Venue

Like all the previous years, the Tournament will take place at the Hauptbücherei, Urban-Loritz-Platz 2a, 1070 Wien.

FAQ

  • How much does it cost?
    • The Tournament is free of charge and has always been.
  • Is the Tournament beginner-friendly?
    • Yes, it is open for anybody who wishes to come, but the registration is required. You are very welcome to join us, even if you have never played a tournament.
  • Are there any age limitations? May I bring my children?
    • We would like to create a convenient environment for our chess players, that is why people under 12 are allowed as participants only. There are no other age limitations.
  • What's the duration of the Tournament?
    • According to our experience, it takes three hours or more depending on the number of participants.
  • Are there any awards?
    • There are no money awards, but all players get participation certificates — and three best players get cup trophies and presents from our partners.
